Why Coastal Living Creates Unique Pest Challenges

  • Salt Air Damage — Corroded seals, warped frames, and degraded caulking create pest entry points faster than inland properties.

  • Mosquito Breeding — Coastal moisture and garden irrigation create standing water that mosquitoes exploit year-round.
  • Cockroach Infestations — Warm, humid conditions accelerate cockroach reproduction cycles, requiring more aggressive treatment.
