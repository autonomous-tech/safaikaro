Why Phase 7 Construction Sites Need Termite Treatment

  • Exposed Soil — Excavation and grading disturb natural soil barriers, giving termites direct access to new foundations.
  • Wooden Formwork — Temporary wooden structures used during construction attract termite colonies to the site.
  • Moisture Buildup — Fresh concrete and irrigation from neighbouring plots create the damp conditions termites thrive in.
  • Neighbouring Colonies — Active construction on adjacent plots can push existing termite colonies toward your foundation.
